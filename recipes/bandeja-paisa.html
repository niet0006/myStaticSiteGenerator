# Bandeja Paisa

A hearty platter that represents the diverse flavors of Colombian cuisine.

# Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef
  • 200g pork belly
  • 2 chorizos
  • 2 fried eggs
  • 1 plantain, sliced
  • 1 cup cooked red beans
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 arepa
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Hogao (Colombian tomato and onion sauce)

# Instructions

  1. Cook the white rice according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, fry the pork belly until crispy.
  3. Fry the chorizos in the same pan.
  4. Fry the plantain slices until golden brown.
  5. In another pan, cook the ground beef.
  6. Serve everything on a large platter, topped with fried eggs and accompanied by arepa, avocado, and hogao.
